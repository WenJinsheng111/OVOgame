What makes the "Random" sports games so popular?

The Random series (Basket Random, Soccer Random, Boxing Random) is popular because every round completely changes the rules. Player sizes, physics, court conditions, and equipment are randomized, creating unpredictable and hilarious moments that keep every match feeling fresh and entertaining.

Can I play sports games with a friend on one computer?

Yes! Most sports and physics games in this collection support 2-player local multiplayer using one keyboard. Player 1 typically uses W or left-side keys, and Player 2 uses the Up Arrow or right-side keys. It is the perfect setup for competitive couch gaming.

Do these physics games work on school Chromebooks?
