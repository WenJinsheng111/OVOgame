What is Boxing Random?

Boxing Random is one of the most entertaining and unpredictable online boxing games you'll ever play. Developed as part of the popular "Random" game series, this physics-based fighting game puts two players in a boxing ring where anything can happen.

What makes Boxing Random truly special is the randomization mechanic. Every round changes the rules — your character's size, weight, arm length, and even the environment can shift dramatically. One moment you might be a tiny boxer with lightning-fast punches, and the next you could be a giant with powerful but slow swings.

The game uses a ragdoll physics engine that creates hilarious and unexpected moments. No two matches are ever the same, which is why millions of players worldwide keep coming back for more. Whether you're playing against a friend or challenging the CPU, every knockout feels earned and every loss is hilariously entertaining.

How to Play Boxing Random

PlayerControlAction
Player 1WPunch / Jump
Player 2Up ArrowPunch / Jump

Boxing Random follows a simple best-of-9 format. Two players face off in a boxing ring, and the goal is to knock your opponent down. Each successful knockdown scores one point. The first player to reach 5 points wins the match.

After each round, the game randomizes everything — character sizes, arm lengths, weights, and sometimes even the playing field itself. Timing is everything — press your key at the right moment to land a devastating punch.
