What is Rooftop Snipers?

Rooftop Snipers is a fast-paced 2-player shooting duel that takes place on the edges of rooftops high above the city. Two players face off in an intense one-on-one battle where the goal is simple: shoot your opponent and knock them off the building. With its simple two-button controls and physics-based knockback system, every shot counts and every match delivers thrilling moments of tension and triumph.

The game uses a physics-based knockback mechanic that makes each hit impactful and unpredictable. When a bullet connects, it pushes your opponent backward toward the edge of the rooftop. Multiple hits in succession can send them flying off completely, but a well-timed jump can save them from falling. This push-and-pull dynamic creates intense back-and-forth exchanges where both players are always one shot away from victory or defeat.

Rooftop Snipers features multiple characters and maps to keep the experience fresh. Each character has a distinct look, and different rooftop locations offer varying widths and layouts that affect gameplay strategy. Wider rooftops give players more room to recover from hits, while narrow ledges make every shot potentially lethal. The variety ensures that matches feel different even after dozens of rounds.

The beauty of Rooftop Snipers lies in its elegant simplicity. With only two buttons per player — jump and shoot — the game is instantly accessible to anyone. But beneath that simplicity is surprising depth. Timing your jumps to dodge incoming shots, angling your aim by jumping at different heights, and reading your opponent's patterns all contribute to a competitive experience that rewards skill and quick thinking. The best-of-9 format (first to 5 wins) ensures that matches are decided by consistent performance rather than lucky shots, making every victory feel well-earned.

How to Play Rooftop Snipers

PlayerControlAction
Player 1WJump
Player 1EShoot
Player 2IJump
Player 2OShoot

Two players stand on opposite ends of a rooftop. Use your jump key to leap into the air and your shoot key to fire at your opponent. Each hit pushes your opponent backward toward the edge. Knock them off the rooftop to score a point. First to 5 points wins the match.

Jumping serves both offensive and defensive purposes. Jump to avoid incoming shots, and shoot while airborne to change your firing angle. The timing between jumping and shooting is key to landing consistent hits.

Tips for Rooftop Snipers

  • Jump before shooting: Shooting while airborne changes your angle and makes you harder to hit. Use short hops for quick shots and big jumps for evasion.
  • Time your shots: Do not spam the shoot button. Wait for your opponent to land or commit to a jump, then fire when they are most vulnerable.
  • Use defensive jumps: When near the edge, jump backward to create distance. A well-timed defensive jump can save you from being knocked off.
  • Read your opponent: Watch for patterns in when they jump and shoot. Most players develop habits you can predict and exploit.
