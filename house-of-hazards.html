What is House of Hazards?

House of Hazards is a hilarious multiplayer game that turns an ordinary household into a battlefield of traps, tricks, and chaos. The concept is brilliantly simple: players take turns trying to complete everyday tasks around the house while other players control environmental hazards to stop them. What results is one of the most entertaining local multiplayer experiences available in your browser.

The gameplay loop in House of Hazards creates a unique asymmetric multiplayer dynamic. When it is your turn, you navigate through different rooms trying to complete objectives like turning on the TV, getting dressed, or making it to the front door. Meanwhile, the other players control various household objects and traps — they can swing doors into your face, drop objects from shelves, activate ceiling fans, and trigger dozens of other creative hazards designed to knock you down and prevent you from completing your tasks.

What makes House of Hazards so special is the reversal of roles each round. The player who was just setting traps now has to navigate through the same hazard-filled house, while their opponents get revenge by controlling the traps. This back-and-forth creates a natural escalation of sabotage as players remember which hazards were most effective and use them against each other. The result is matches that build in intensity and hilarity as players learn each other's strategies.

The game supports 2 to 3 players locally, making it perfect for playing with friends or siblings. Each room in the house presents different hazards and tasks, keeping the gameplay varied and unpredictable. The cartoon art style and exaggerated physics add to the comedic tone, with characters ragdolling comically when hit by traps. Whether you are the one dodging falling lamps and slamming doors or the one orchestrating the chaos, House of Hazards guarantees laughter and memorable gaming moments that make you want to play round after round.

Players take turns being the active player who must complete tasks, and the hazard controller who triggers traps. As the active player, move through rooms and interact with highlighted objectives. As the hazard controller, click on glowing objects to trigger traps when the active player is nearby.

Each round, the active player has a set of tasks to complete before time runs out. The player who completes the most tasks across all rounds wins. Timing your trap activations for maximum impact is key to stopping your opponents.

Tips for House of Hazards

  • Watch for glowing objects: As the active player, look for highlighted hazard points. Knowing where traps are helps you time your movements to avoid them.
  • Be patient with traps: As the hazard controller, wait until the active player is directly in range before triggering. Premature activation wastes the trap's cooldown.
  • Move unpredictably: Stop, start, and change direction to bait your opponents into triggering traps early when you are not in the danger zone.
  • Prioritize nearby tasks: Complete tasks that are close together first to minimize your exposure to hazards while moving between rooms.
